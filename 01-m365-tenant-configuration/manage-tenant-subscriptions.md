# Manage Tenant Subscriptions in Microsoft 365

---

## 🔑 Key Concepts (Must Know)

### What is Subscription Management?

Subscription management involves:

- Monitoring licenses  
- Assigning/removing licenses  
- Purchasing additional licenses  
- Managing billing and payments  

👉 It ensures users have access to services without overspending.

---

## ⚠️ Why It Matters

- Too few licenses → users cannot access services  
- Too many licenses → unnecessary cost  

👉 Real-world:
License shortages can cause **provisioning errors** and onboarding delays.

---

## 📦 License Availability (CRITICAL)

If all licenses are assigned:

- New users cannot be licensed  
- Group-based licensing fails  
- Errors occur during provisioning  

👉 Example:
New starter cannot access email because no licenses are available

---

## ⚙️ Where to Manage Subscriptions

👉 **Microsoft 365 Admin Center**

Path:
Billing → Licenses → Subscriptions

---

## 💰 Billing Behaviour (IMPORTANT)

When you purchase additional licenses:

- Billing date may change  
- Multiple billing cycles can exist  

👉 Example:
- Original subscription → 14th  
- New licenses added → 15th  
- Result → multiple billing dates  

---

## 🔄 License Lifecycle (Real-World Flow)

### New User
- Create user  
- Assign license  

---

### User Role Change
- Modify license  
- Add/remove services  

---

### User Leaves
- Remove license  
- Reassign to another user  

---

## 🧠 Group-Based Licensing (Important)

- Licenses assigned via groups  
- Automatic license assignment  

👉 Risk:
If licenses run out → provisioning errors

---

## 🔥 Interview Questions

### Q1: What happens if you run out of licenses?

👉 Answer:
Users cannot be assigned licenses, and provisioning errors may occur, especially with group-based licensing.

---

### Q2: Where do you check license availability?

👉 Answer:
In the Microsoft 365 Admin Center under Billing → Licenses.

---

### Q3: How do you manage subscription costs?

👉 Answer:
By monitoring license usage, removing unused licenses, and ensuring the correct plan is assigned to users.

---

### Q4: What is group-based licensing?

👉 Answer:
It is a method of assigning licenses automatically to users based on their group membership.
